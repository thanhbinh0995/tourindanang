Da Nang Golfing Experience

from $129 Day tour    Da Nang,

Combine your vacation in Da Nang with an enjoyable half day experience golfing at one of the golf courses in Hoi An/Da Nang. Only few km from Da Nang/Hoi An City, the Montgomerie Links Vietnam Golf and the Danang Golf Club welcome you with two 18-holes magnificent courses.

Tour Itinerary

Da Nang Golf Club is characterized by wild seaside vegetation and many sand dunes, with a superb view on the ocean while playing. The Montgomerie Links Vietnam Golf was selected as the best golf course in Vietnam in 2010 by the magazine Asian Golf. The course is characterized by a striking landscape of wispy casuarinas pines and sand dunes. You will challenge yourself in one 18-hole round at Da Nang Golf Club or Montgomerie Links Golf Course.
