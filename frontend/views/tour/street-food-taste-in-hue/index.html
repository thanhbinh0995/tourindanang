Street food taste in Hue

from $45 Day tour    Hue,

Hue, the capital of Vietnam during the Nguyen dynasty (1802-1945), known for the very delicate and diverse cuisine, royal food. This food tour takes you to taste some of the best Hue local food, street food and you also explore diversity of culture of Hue city, the imperial capital of Vietnam. This is should-not-miss experience for all travelers to Hue.

Tour Itinerary

Duration: 4 hours.
Departure time: 9:00am or 3:00pm
Pickup location: Centrally located hotels in Hue City.

Taste Hue local street food

Start from your hotel on a cyclo ride to cross over the historical bridge Trang Tien to visit the colorful Dong Ba market where you can see a lot of ingredients for making Hue local food and explore the business life of the local. Then, continue along busy streets to see the ordinary life of the local which most tourists don’t get to see. And ride around the royal palace and take pictures at beautiful Hue historical sites and listen to stories about food, royal cuisines and imperial history which our guide will be sharing. During the tour, you will stop at many famous street food stalls to enjoy a lot of Hue local street food:

Banh Khoai – Hue pancake, Bun Bo Hue – Hue beef vermicelli soup, Banh Loc – Filtered tapioca dumplings, Banh Beo – Steamed rice discs topped with shrimp, Banh Nam – Steamed flat rice dumplings, Banh Uot – Hue wet rice paper, Bun Thit Nuong – Grilled pork vermicelli, Nem Lui – Grilled chopped meat, Banh La Gai – Pinnate leaf cake, Me Xung – Sesame candy…

Before ending the tour, you also choose to stop at a corner coffee shop and watch life going on.
