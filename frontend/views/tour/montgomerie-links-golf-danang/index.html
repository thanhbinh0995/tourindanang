Tour Itinerary

Montgomerie Links is located midway between Hoi An and Danang. It is just 15 minutes drive from Danang International Airport, which is serviced by direct local and international flights.

The course boasts a stunning 7100-yard, par-72 championship layout. Its designer is the 2010 victorious European Ryder Cup captain and eight-time European Tour Order of Merit winner Colin Montgomerie, known as ‘Monty’ to his worldwide fan base. The over-arching design philosophy behind a Colin Montgomerie golf course is to work closely with the natural characteristics of the site. Montgomerie Links lives up to this expectation with massaged massive greens and rippled fairways set among a stunning backdrop.

Acclaimed by local and visiting golfers alike, it is challenging enough to test the best players, while being eminently playable and enjoyable for mid and higher handicappers.
