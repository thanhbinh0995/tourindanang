Dinner Cruise on Han River

from $15 Day tour    Da Nang,

At night, when the lights go on, Da Nang becomes a colorful and vibrant city, sparkling by the famous and unique bridges, light reflection on the Han River. Soak up at night life of Da Nang city, having dinner on a cruise going along Han River, seeing different Da Nang from another side, admire these sparkling bridges.. this would be your unique experience in Da Nang.

Tour Itinerary

Dinner on boat cruising Han River, Da Nang

17.30 pick up at your hotel, transfer to the wharf. Board the cruise ship, enjoy welcome drink.

18.00 Start sailing and admire the colorful, lights on city, sparkling bridges:

– Han River Bridge – the only swing bridge in Vietnam

– Thuan Phuoc Bridge – the longest cable-stayed bridge in Vietnam

– Dragon Bridge – the world’s longest iron dragon.

– Tran Thi Ly Bridge – unique leaning piller, cable-stayed system as sailing out to the sea.

Buffet dinner (or set dinner) while enjoying performance on board.

You also have chance to see the Iron Dragon spits fire (Weekend – Saturday & Sunday)

21.00 End the tour at the wharf. Car await to take you back to your hotel.
